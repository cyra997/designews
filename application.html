Application development

Developing IOS Photo editor for blind or visually impaired people.

The application is designed for blind and visually impaired people to take their own photos, edit them and then upload it to their social media accounts. The app speaks to the user and describes what is on the screen. Depending on what the user wants to do, they can tap on the correct part of the screen which will take them to the next step. The user will have their picture uploaded to their chosen account.
